About Allison.

Who? What? When? Where? How?
A (temporary) silly text.

Who is Allison? Born in Atlanta, Georgia, Allison then chose to move to Brussels, Belgium at the ripe old age of one-and-a-half. She grew up surrounded by Belgians, waffles, woods, colored pencils and storybooks. After, for some unknown reason, achieving a glamorous degree in Economics and Management, she opted to follow her dream of studying art, which is what she's now doing in Ghent (that's also in Belgium, folks).

In her free time, Allison enjoys playing the ukulele, knitting brightly colored socks, walking among trees, baking and reading.
